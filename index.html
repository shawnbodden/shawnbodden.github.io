Freelance Work

In addition to my academic research, I have experience leading participatory evaluation research for a range of community groups and cultural organisations. The aim of my work is to facilitate dialogue between the different participants of community projects about their experiences, different perspectives, and lessons-learned for future work. I believe evaluation should be a generative and empowering process, and I can support this aim in a number of ways:

  • Narrative Case Studies & Project Reports
  • Reflective Practice Workshops
  • Co-writing Facilitation & Developmental Editing
  • Graphic Design & Illustration

If you would be interested in working together, please get in touch.

Blog & Atlas

This year, I'm starting a new blog about community spaces and the work people do to keep them open. I'll be sharing stories about specific places and reflections on the different ways community workers, policymakers, and researchers have tried to describe what it is these spaces do. It will be a little bit geography, a little bit illustration, and a lot of places to be.

Read the latest!

Later down the line, I'll start gathering these posts into an Atlas of Community Spaces. This will tag the places I've written about, but also allow me to compile information and bibliographies about specific types of community spaces. If you have any thoughts or questions about the project, send me a message.
